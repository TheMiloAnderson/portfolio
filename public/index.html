About Me

In my younger days, back home in Alaska, I used to do residential construction work, and some of the same skills and attitudes have served me well in web production environments: work hard; plan ahead; think in three dimensions; measure twice.

As with construction, the most crucial consideration in building information systems is usability -- human usability. Properly considered, users aren't separate from the system; they are an essential component.
